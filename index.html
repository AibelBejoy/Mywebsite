Indoor plants

Snake Plant: This hardy plant thrives in low light and requires minimal watering, making it perfect for forgetful plant owners.
Peace Lily: Known for its elegant white flowers, the Peace Lily also purifies the air and prefers indirect light and moist soil.
Spider Plant: With its arching leaves and air-purifying qualities, the Spider Plant is easy to grow and produces baby plants that can be propagated.
ZZ Plant: The ZZ Plant is highly drought-tolerant, prefers low to bright indirect light, and is virtually indestructible, making it ideal for beginners.
